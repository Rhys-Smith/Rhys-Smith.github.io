What is the difference between Margin, Border, and Padding?

Margins, Borders and padding

  • A margin can be best described as an area which seperates things like paragrahs
    ,headers,images from each other.
  • Borders wraps around content on your webpage such as images and text.
  • Padding allows you to controll how much space there is between content and borders.

    • Examples

    When to use

  • Use margin to space your content.
  • Use borders to outline your images.
  • use padding to add spacing from the margine and your content.
