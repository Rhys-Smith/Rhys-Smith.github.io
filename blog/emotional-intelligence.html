Research EQ and IQ:

is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence is the abillity to understand, notice and use not just your emotions but everyone around you.

How is it different to IQ?

IQ is based apon ones abillity to pick up information, to make logically decisions,actions or problem solve
EQ is based apon ones abillity to recognize emotion not just with yourself, but within others around you

Why is it important?

Understanding the importance of EQ not only helps you, but its for the betterment of everyone around you.
Understanding access,expression,processes and identification of not only problems but eachothers wellbeing sets a foundation for success.
Promote and create useful mental habits to grasp a better understand of yourself and those around you.
